01 · Anatomy

The mark, decoded

Three components, each carrying meaning. The Y is the pivot — both inside the mark (carried by the centre) and inside the wordmark (the only amber letter).

Six dots
Family members. Hexagonal arrangement reads as balance — no member subordinate to another.
Amber centre
The user — adult caregiver at the heart, holding the V of Vitalynx. The pivot of the family.
Dashed orbit
The shared connection. Dashed rather than solid — relationships are continuous, not rigid.
